Meet Vanessa

With over 11 years of experience, I've had the privilege of walking alongside hundreds of clients through their most difficult moments. My philosophy is simple: I'm here to guide, not to judge.

I believe my ideal client is someone who is motivated for change and ready to learn new approaches to thinking and behavior. Counseling isn't about having someone fix you. It's about having support as you discover your own strength.

"Counseling allows us to take better care of ourselves, our relationships, and our families."

Whether we meet in my office, online, or on a walking path, my goal is to provide a respectful, nurturing environment where real healing can happen.
